Our Story

Ceg Designs was founded in 2018 in the heart of Cardiff with a simple mission: to deliver exceptional graphic design that makes a real difference for local businesses. What started as a small studio in Cardiff Bay has grown into one of South Wales' most respected creative agencies.

Our name, "Ceg" — meaning "mouth" in Welsh — reflects our belief that great design speaks volumes. We give businesses a visual voice that cuts through the noise and connects with the people who matter most.

Today, our team of six talented creatives work with businesses of all sizes across Cardiff, Newport, Swansea, and beyond. From independent high street shops to growing tech start-ups, we bring the same passion, attention to detail and strategic thinking to every brief.

We're proud to be part of the Cardiff creative community. We regularly collaborate with local photographers, copywriters and developers, and we believe that investing in Welsh talent helps the whole region thrive.

The Team

Meet the Creatives

A close-knit team of designers, strategists and problem-solvers who care deeply about great work.

RH

Rhys Hughes

Creative Director & Founder

With over 15 years in the industry, Rhys founded Ceg Designs to bring world-class creative to Welsh businesses. A Cardiff Met graduate with a passion for brand storytelling, he leads the creative vision across all projects.

ME

Megan Evans

Senior Graphic Designer

Megan specialises in brand identity and print design. After honing her skills at agencies in London and Bristol, she returned to Cardiff to join Ceg Designs in 2020. Her meticulous eye for detail brings every project to life.

TJ

Tom Jenkins

Web Designer & Developer

Tom bridges the gap between design and code. A self-taught developer turned designer, he creates responsive websites that are as beautiful as they are functional. Tom has been with the team since 2019.

SP

Sian Price

Digital Marketing Designer

Sian creates scroll-stopping social media content and digital campaigns. With a background in marketing and a degree in illustration from USW, she understands both the creative and strategic sides of digital.

OD

Owen Davies

Junior Designer

Owen joined the team in 2024 after graduating from Cardiff University. He brings fresh perspectives and boundless energy to every project, with a keen interest in motion graphics and packaging design.

CL

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2018

Founded in Cardiff Bay

Rhys Hughes launches Ceg Designs from a shared workspace in Cardiff Bay, taking on freelance branding and print work for local businesses.

2019

First Full-Time Hire

Tom Jenkins joins as web designer, expanding our services to include website design and development. We move into our first proper studio.

2020

Growing Through Challenge

Despite the pandemic, we double our client base by pivoting to digital-first services. Megan Evans joins the team from Bristol.

2022

Award-Winning Work

We pick up our first industry awards, including Best Brand Identity at the Welsh Design Awards for our work with Cymru Coffee.

2024

Team of Six

The team grows to six with the additions of Sian, Owen and Carys. We relocate to a larger studio at Neptune Court in Cardiff.

2026

Looking Ahead

With over 350 projects delivered, we continue to grow while staying true to our roots: crafting bold, purposeful design for businesses that want to stand out.
